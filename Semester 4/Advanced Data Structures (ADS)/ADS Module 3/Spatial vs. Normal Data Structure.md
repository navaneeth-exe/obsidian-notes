|Feature|Normal Data Structures|Spatial Data Structures|
|---|---|---|
|**Definition**|Data structures used to store and manage **linear or simple data**.|Data structures used to store and manage **spatial or multi-dimensional data**.|
|**Type of Data**|Stores **numbers, characters, or records**.|Stores **geometric data such as points, lines, rectangles, and polygons**.|
|**Dimensions**|Mostly deals with **one-dimensional data**.|Handles **two-dimensional or multi-dimensional data**.|
|**Searching**|Searches based on **value or key**.|Searches based on **spatial location or coordinates**.|
|**Examples**|Array, Linked List, Stack, Queue, Binary Tree.|K-d Tree, R-tree, Quad Tree, Octree.|
|**Applications**|General data processing and algorithm implementation.|GIS, computer graphics, image processing, and spatial databases.|
